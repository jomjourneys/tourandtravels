Srinagar and Kashmir Valley Exploration

5 Nights 6 Days Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Arrive at Srinagar, where you'll be greeted and transferred to your hotel. Begin with a visit to Dal Lake for a serene shikara ride. Explore Mughal Gardens like Shalimar Bagh and Nishat Bagh. End the day with a local market stroll, absorbing the vibrant culture and shopping for souvenirs.

Day 2: Srinagar to Gulmarg

Drive to Gulmarg, renowned for its lush meadows and stunning views. Take the Gulmarg Gondola to Apharwat Peak for panoramic vistas. In winter, enjoy skiing and other snow activities. Conclude the day with a peaceful walk through the meadows, taking in the fresh mountain air.

Day 3: Gulmarg to Pahalgam

Travel to Pahalgam, known for its picturesque landscapes. Visit Betaab Valley and Aru Valley, ideal for trekking and enjoying nature. Explore the beautiful riverside and relax amid the tranquil setting. Pahalgamâ€™s natural beauty offers a perfect respite from urban life.

Day 4: Pahalgam Exploration

Explore Pahalgam further with activities like horse riding, trekking, or river rafting. Visit local attractions like the Lidder River and Baisaran Valley, also known as mini-Switzerland. Enjoy a picnic surrounded by nature or simply relax and admire the stunning views.

Day 5: Pahalgam to Sonamarg

Drive to Sonamarg, known as the 'Meadow of Gold'. Visit Thajiwas Glacier, where you can engage in snow activities like sledging. Enjoy pony rides or trekking to nearby scenic spots. The journey to Sonamarg is as enchanting as the destination itself.

Day 6: Departure from Srinagar

Return to Srinagar for your departure. Depending on your flight schedule, you might have time for some last-minute shopping or sightseeing. Explore the old city for its rich heritage before transferring to the airport with lasting memories of Kashmir.
