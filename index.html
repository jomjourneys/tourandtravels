Trending Destinations

Kashmir

Explore the vibrant charm of Srinagar, Kashmir's heart. Wander through its ancient streets, adorned with Mughal gardens and historic monuments. Experience the city's rich culture and warm hospitality, where every corner tells a story of timeless beauty.

Gulmarg

Experience the exhilarating beauty of Gulmarg, Kashmir's winter paradise. Embrace the thrill of skiing down pristine slopes, surrounded by majestic snow-capped peaks. Let Gulmarg's natural wonders captivate your senses, promising an unforgettable journey into pure bliss.

Pahalgam

Pahalgam, nestled in the Himalayas, enchants with its picturesque valleys and gushing rivers. Immerse in nature's splendor through trekking and rafting adventures. Let Pahalgam's serene beauty captivate your soul, promising a journey of unforgettable moments.

Sonamarg

Sonamarg, a Himalayan gem, captivates with lush meadows and towering glaciers. Explore its pristine beauty through trekking and camping. Embrace adventure amidst nature's grandeur, creating memories that linger long after the journey ends.

Dodhpatri

Discover the hidden gem of Dodhpatri, a paradise nestled in the Kashmir Valley. Marvel at its lush meadows, pristine streams, and dense forests. Embark on nature walks and picnics amidst breathtaking landscapes, experiencing tranquility like never before.

Manisbal

Manasbal, a serene gem in Kashmir, captivates with its tranquil waters and surrounding greenery. Enjoy peaceful boat rides on the lake, soaking in the beauty of the Himalayas. Explore nearby orchards and villages, immersing in the region's rich culture and natural splendor.
