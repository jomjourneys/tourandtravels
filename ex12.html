Kashmir Family Package
Duration: 6 Days

Destinations: Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Arrive in Srinagar and transfer to your family-friendly accommodation. Enjoy a scenic Shikara ride on Dal Lake and explore the local markets for souvenirs and crafts.

Day 2: Srinagar Exploration

Visit the beautiful Mughal Gardens, including Shalimar Bagh and Nishat Bagh. Enjoy a family picnic in the gardens and explore the local attractions such as the old city and local bazaars.

Day 3: Srinagar to Gulmarg

Travel to Gulmarg and check into your accommodation. Enjoy a fun-filled day at the Gulmarg Gondola, the worldâ€™s highest cable car, and take in the stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Day 4: Gulmarg Exploration

Spend the day exploring Gulmarg with family-friendly activities. Engage in a nature walk, visit the historic St. Mary's Church, and enjoy the beautiful meadows. Winter activities may include skiing or snowboarding.

Day 5: Gulmarg to Pahalgam

Drive to Pahalgam and settle into your family accommodation. Explore the scenic beauty of Pahalgam with activities such as a visit to Betaab Valley or a stroll along the Lidder River.

Day 6: Departure from Srinagar

Return to Srinagar for your departure. Enjoy some last-minute shopping or a final meal before heading to the airport.
